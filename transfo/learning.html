New Course! Discover Digital for Executives

Learn more!

This course, offered through the CSPS Digital Academy, introduces public service executives to the Government of Canada’s Digital Standards and looks at the impact of new and emerging approaches to doing business.

Participants will have an opportunity to explore the meaning of digital in a government context, build their digital toolbox and experiment towards creating a digitally enabled work environment in their own organizations.
