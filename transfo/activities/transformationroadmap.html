May 2019 - Government of Canada Digital Standards

Learn more!

In September 2018, the Government of Canada (GC) released the first iteration of the GC Digital Standards to guide the delivery of quality digital services to Canadians. The 10 standards form the foundation for how government will shift towards becoming more agile, open, and user-focused. What does this mean for Transport Canada? In May, the Digital Roadmap team will highlight the 10 GC Digital Standards and how they are successfully being applied across TC.


April 2019 - AGILE Approach

Learn more!

You may have heard the term “agile” before. But what does it mean in today’s workplace? In April, the Transformation Roadmap team will explore all the facets of being agile. We’ll discuss how to work collaboratively while keeping users and clients at the heart of what we do. Learning will focus on defining agile, and will showcase projects across TC that have applied the agile method successfully.
